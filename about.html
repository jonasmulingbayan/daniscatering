About Us

Dani's Catering and Events is greatly inspired by my late father, Danilo Ignacio, who became my greatest influence in the kitchen and food business. My dad introduced me to the wonderful world of culinary when I was still a kid. He often tags me along whenever he goes to work for some hotels, restaurants and resorts. Since then, I had this fascination of the things he does in the kitchen. I always watch him cook and prepare dishes. I'd love to mimic his every move and cooking style. We, at Dani's Catering and Events, are always making our clients and guests our topmost priority. We value your welfare and your satisfaction is our natural high! We hope that this would be the beginning of our story with you and your family. God bless us all!
